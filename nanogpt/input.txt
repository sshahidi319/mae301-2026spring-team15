[TITLE]: Simple Scrambled Eggs
[INGREDIENTS]:
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 pinch salt
- 1 pinch black pepper
[DIRECTIONS]:
1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk until combined.
2. Melt the butter in a small skillet over medium-low heat.
3. Pour in the eggs and let them sit for 20 seconds.
4. Gently stir with a spatula until soft curds form.
5. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.

[TITLE]: Quick Garlic Pasta
[INGREDIENTS]:
- 8 oz spaghetti
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
[DIRECTIONS]:
1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
3. Add garlic and red pepper flakes; sauté for 1 minute.
4. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
5. Toss the pasta in the garlic oil, adding pasta water as needed.
6. Top with parmesan cheese and serve.

[TITLE]: Classic Peanut Butter Toast
[INGREDIENTS]:
- 2 slices whole wheat bread
- 2 tbsp peanut butter
- 1 banana, sliced
- 1 tsp honey
[DIRECTIONS]:
1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
2. Spread a thick layer of peanut butter on each slice.
3. Arrange the banana slices on top of the peanut butter.
4. Drizzle with honey and enjoy.

[TITLE]: Basic Garden Salad
[INGREDIENTS]:
- 4 cups mixed greens
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinaigrette
[DIRECTIONS]:
1. Wash and dry the mixed greens.
2. In a large bowl, combine the greens, cucumber, and onion.
3. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette and toss gently to coat.
